Understanding and Comparing Certain Privacy Laws

GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA constitute the most rigorous sets of laws, policies, and directives issued by various government authorities worldwide, designed to prioritize and protect consumer data. The guidelines and directives issued under these acts & policies cover several jurisdictions. They comprehensively cover data collection, storage, usage, trading, and access practices with an impetus of keeping the consumer in the loop before his/her data is used for profit-seeking purposes.
