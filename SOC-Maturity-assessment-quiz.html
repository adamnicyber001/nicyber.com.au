SOC Maturity Assessment

Home > Resources > SOC Maturity Assessment
Facebook Twitter Instagram Mail

Cyber-attacks can have a massive impact on any company. As per Cybersecurity Venture’s 2020 report, cyberattacks are projected to hit $6 Trillion in annual losses in 2021 which is a number that has doubled since 2015. A cyber-incident can negatively influence customer confidence and ruin the brand reputation of a company built over many years.

With advancements in technology, security is becoming increasingly complex. In order to manage these risks, it is imperative that organization adopt continuous security monitoring. This is best achieved via a Security Operations Centre (SOC) that could be either in-house or completely outsourced.

Network Intelligence's SOC Maturity model is a vendor-agnostic and SIEM-agnostic self-assessment tool. It has been developed based on our experience of managing SOCs for clients across industries and geographies for more than a decade.

This quiz enables you to self-assess the current state of your SOC. Based on the score, it provides pointers to improve the SOC's performance so that you can detect threats and respond to them faster, thereby enhancing your organization’s cyber resilience.
