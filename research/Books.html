Metasploit Framework - Syngress Publishing
Co-authored by K. K. Mookhey, this book is one of the first publications on the latest version of the Metasploit Framework

This is the first book available for the Metasploit Framework (MSF), which is the attack platform of choice for one of the fastest growing careers in IT security: Penetration Testing. The book and companion Web site will provide professional penetration testers and security researchers with a fully integrated suite of tools for discovering, running, and testing exploit code.


Linux Security, Audit and Control Features, published by ISACA
Commission by ISACA, this book is a comprehensive guide on Linux Security and Controls, authored by K. K. Mookhey and Nilesh Burghate

"To help security professionals put this material to use immediately at their companies, we have based many of the recommendations on our own practical experience working with many large and medium enterprises," said the book's author, K. K. Mookhey, founder-CTO, Network Intelligence India Pvt Ltd. "Readers are introduced to several types of Linux deployments at a hypothetical organization, XYZ Inc., including gateways, firewalls, web servers, databases and file servers."
